Data Sources

All machine data, metrics, and statistics displayed in the App are sourced from the vast.ai API. We do not generate, verify, or guarantee the accuracy of this data.

Statistics Are Estimates

Runtime statistics and metrics provided by the App are estimates based on:

  • App polling intervals (typically 15-20 minutes)
  • Network availability and latency
  • API response times and reliability
  • Local device caching

Known Limitations

  • Runtime Statistics: Accuracy of ±5-15 minutes due to polling intervals
  • Temperature Readings: Provided by vast.ai; we do not verify accuracy or calibration
  • Performance Metrics: Based on vast.ai data; may vary from actual performance
  • Reliability Scores: Calculated by vast.ai, not by Fleet GPU
